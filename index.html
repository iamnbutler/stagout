TORONTO HIP-HOP DUO

Sandy Pearlman and Bones (AKA - Pearls & Bones) are a Toronto based hip hop duo. The group focuses on intricate lyricism courtesy of Bones mixed with innovative beats that prove genre is no barrier provided by producer and occasional vocalist Sandy Pearlman. The groups progression has skyrocketed enabling them to record in Toronto's award winning studio Revolution Recording in preparation for their debut full length album. Stage shattering shows with the likes of Machine Gun Kelly and Tyga have proved that originality and energy will continually manifest a strong, appreciated and involved fan base. After a successful underground EP release for the duo Bones is in the process of finalizing the design of the album drop, from merch, to album art, and everything in between. The movements fan support has created a buzz bubbling for the release. It's time to Stag Out or get out of the way!
